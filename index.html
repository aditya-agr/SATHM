Welcome to SATHM

The School of Tourism, Aviation, and Hospitality Management (SATHM) is a premier institution dedicated to delivering top-notch education in the fields of aviation, tourism, and hospitality management. Established in July 2024 under the esteemed Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), SATHM is designed to meet the rapidly growing demands of these dynamic industries.

Our vision is to bridge the academia-industry gap by offering skill-oriented programs in partnership with industry leaders including Airport Authority of India, MP Flying Club, and National Skill Development Corporation.

Message from the Head of Department

At SATHM, we are committed to nurturing professionals who are industry-ready, ethically grounded, and globally competent. Our curriculum blends academic rigor with experiential learning through apprenticeships, internships, and industry collaborations with partners like Airport Authority of India, MP Flying Club, and leading corporations.

We provide holistic development by giving students platforms to participate in inter-college activities like sports, cultural clubs, community services, and field visits for greater academic achievement. We welcome you to embark on a transformative journey with us.

Dr. Yamini Karmakar
Head (SATHM)

Mission and Vision

Our Vision

To be a premier school for Aviation, Tourism and Hospitality education, fostering innovation, employability, and entrepreneurship to serve the evolving service economy.

Our Mission

  • Industry Integration: Partner with leading organizations to provide hands-on learning.
  • Skill Development: Deliver competency-based curricula aligned with National Education Policy (NEP).
  • Holistic Growth: Cultivate leadership, communication, and problem-solving abilities.
  • Global Outlook: Encourage research, innovation, and entrepreneurial thinking.
