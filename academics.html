Academic Programs

Discover our range of specialized programs designed for industry success.

Undergraduate Programs

BBA (Aviation Management)

Duration: 3 Years

Aims to develop a strong foundation in aviation operations, airline management, and airport logistics, while equipping students with essential regulatory knowledge.

B.Com (Retail Operations)

Duration: 3 Years

An apprenticeship-embedded program to integrate academic learning with real-world retail experience through structured on-the-job training.

B.Com (Logistics)

Duration: 3 Years

This program blends theoretical knowledge with practical apprenticeship experience, equipping students with essential skills in supply chain management.

Postgraduate Program

MBA (Tourism)

Duration: 2 Years

Designed to develop strategic leadership skills for managing tourism businesses, travel agencies, and hospitality ventures, with a focus on destination management and sustainable tourism.

Integrated Program

MBA (Tourism) - Integrated

Duration: 5 Years

Offers a comprehensive, industry-aligned curriculum that seamlessly integrates undergraduate and postgraduate learning in tourism. Provides multi-disciplinary exposure to tourism policy, heritage management, and event planning.

Diploma Program

Diploma in Aviation Security

Duration: 1.5 Years

Designed to train professionals in aviation safety, security procedures, and regulatory compliance, with a focus on passenger screening, risk assessment, and crisis management.
