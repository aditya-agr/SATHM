Industry Collaborations & MOUs

Bridging the gap between academia and industry through strategic partnerships.

MP Flying Club, Indore

Strategic partnership to provide students with specialized, hands-on training in the aviation sector including practical flight operations and aviation management exposure.

Airport Authority of India (AAI) - New Delhi and Indore

Comprehensive MoU for training students in specific skills related to airport operations, ground handling, cargo management, and aviation security protocols.

National Development Sector-Skill Council (NSDC), Government of India

Partnership to align curriculum with national skill development initiatives and provide industry-recognized certifications to enhance student employability.

Logistics Sector Skill Council (LSC)

Tripartite agreement between Commissioner of College Education (First Party), Logistics Sector Skill Council (Second Party), and Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (Third Party) for apprenticeship-based programs in logistics and supply chain management.

Retailers Association Skill Council of India (RASCI)

Collaboration to deliver industry-vetted curriculum and provide assured paid internships in retail operations, enhancing practical skills and employability for students.

MOU Objectives

Internship Programs

Provide practical training and exposure to students through structured internship programs with industry partners, ensuring hands-on experience in real work environments.

Skill Development

Create interest in Civil Aviation among students by supplementing theoretical knowledge with practical operations and giving exposure in STEM/non-STEM domains.

Project-Based Learning

Provide opportunities for students to work on small-duration projects related to Civil Aviation, Tourism, and Hospitality management.

Financial Support

Ensure financial assistance and support for students during their apprenticeship and internship programs to promote inclusive learning opportunities.
